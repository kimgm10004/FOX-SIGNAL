# Lotto Number Generator

## Overview

A simple, visually appealing web application to generate random lottery numbers.

##- Design and Features (Current)

- **UI:**
    - Centered layout.
    - Title "Lotto Number Generator".
    - A display area for the generated numbers.
    - A "Generate" button.
- **Functionality:**
    - Click the "Generate" button to produce 6 unique random numbers between 1 and 45.
    - The generated numbers are displayed in styled circles.
